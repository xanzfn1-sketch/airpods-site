Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our local delivery.

How fast is the local delivery? +

We operate a dedicated same-day delivery network across the BB1 and BB2 areas. Most orders arrive right to your door within 45-60 minutes of placing your order on WhatsApp.

How do I pay for my order? +

Once you submit your basket, it sends a direct automated message to our WhatsApp line. We will confirm your delivery address there and arrange your preferred payment option (Cash on Delivery or Bank Transfer).

What is the return/exchange policy? +

We want to make sure you are 100% satisfied. If there is any manufacturer defect or connection issue with your pairs, just message us within 7 days for a completely free local replacement drop-off.

Do you offer wholesale bulk discounts? +

Yes! Our Bulk Deal package is listed directly in the store basket, but if you want to order larger amounts (20+ pairs) for resale, message us directly via the WhatsApp link to negotiate custom tier rates.
